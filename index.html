About Me

I'm a California native currently living and working in Amsterdam. Since graduating from UC Berkeley with a degree in City Planning and GIS, I've been working the last 4 years as a Data Analyst & GIS Specialist at the Netherlands based mobility company Goudappel. My professional work has focused on translating best Dutch transportation strategies to the international cities.

When I'm not making maps for work, I'm making maps for side projects, conducting my own open source investigations and exploring the world through numbers and visualizations.

After I close the computer for the day, you can find me biking into headwinds, playing drums, or jumping into cold bodies of water.
